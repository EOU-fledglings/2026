---

## üé§ Oral Presentations

Please note the following guidelines for oral presentations:

- **Presentation length:**  
  - **10 minutes** 
- **Discussion:**  
  - **4 minutes** for questions and discussion

Due to the high number of presentations, we will **strictly adhere to the schedule**.  
We therefore strongly recommend rehearsing your talk in advance to ensure it fits within the allocated time.

### Slides (recommended format)

If you use slides, we suggest:

- **Aspect ratio:** 16:9  
- **File format:** `.pdf` or `.pptx`  

This will help avoid technical issues and ensure smooth transitions between speakers.
